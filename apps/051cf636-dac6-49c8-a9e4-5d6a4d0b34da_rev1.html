THE BAND

What We Play

60s through today including Mellencamp, The Doors, Poison, T-Rex, Guns&Roses, Smash Mouth, Trooper, AC/DC and so much more.

We are a group of passionate musicians dedicated to bringing high-energy performances and a diverse range of classic hits to every event. Our goal is to get you dancing and singing along all night long!
