Our Story

Welcome to Bella Italia, where we bring the true taste of Italy to Manhattan. Founded by the Romano family, our restaurant is a celebration of traditional Italian cuisine, passed down through generations.

We use only the freshest ingredients, sourced locally and imported from Italy, to create dishes that are both authentic and unforgettable. From classic pasta dishes to wood-fired pizzas, every bite tells a story of passion and heritage.
