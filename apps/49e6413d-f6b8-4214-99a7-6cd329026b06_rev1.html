About Us

Our Story

Born from a shared love for classic rock anthems and spontaneous jam sessions by the lake, the Lake Tyee Lot Owners Band officially formed in 2022. What started as a casual get-together of music-loving neighbors quickly evolved into a tight-knit group dedicated to recreating the magic of rock's golden era.

From local community events to private parties, we bring passion, energy, and a whole lot of fun to every stage we play. Our sound is rooted in the iconic hits of the 60s, 70s, and 80s, delivered with our own unique flair. We're more than just a band; we're friends, neighbors, and fellow music enthusiasts committed to keeping the spirit of classic rock alive.
