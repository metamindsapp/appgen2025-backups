Our Story

More Than Just Coffee & Croissants

At Kitten Kaboodle, nestled in the heart of Paris, we've brewed up a unique blend of comfort, companionship, and caffeine. Our dream was to create a serene sanctuary where Parisians and travelers alike can unwind in the delightful company of our friendly rescue cats.

We are passionate about animal welfare and providing a loving, stimulating environment for our feline residents. Each cat at Kitten Kaboodle is a cherished member of our family, enjoying a life of leisure, play, and endless affection. We also partner with local shelters to help our adoptable cats find their forever homes.

Join us for artisanal coffee, delectable French pastries, and the soothing purrs of our adorable cats. It's an experience that will warm your heart and lift your spirits.

Café Etiquette (For Happy Cats & Guests)

  • Please sanitize hands upon entry.
  • Speak softly and move calmly.
  • Let cats approach you; don't force interaction.
  • Do not pick up the cats unless invited by staff.
  • No flash photography, please.
  • Do not feed human food to the cats.
  • Children must be supervised by an adult at all times.
