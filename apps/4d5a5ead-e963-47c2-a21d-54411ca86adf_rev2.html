About Zorbas

Nestled in the vibrant Kerstraat 12, Antwerp, Zorbas brings the sun-kissed flavors of Greece to your table. Our passion is to share the rich culinary heritage of Greece, using only the freshest ingredients and time-honored family recipes.

From sizzling souvlaki to creamy moussaka and delicate baklava, every dish is a celebration of authentic Greek taste. Join us for an unforgettable dining experience where warm hospitality meets exquisite food, making you feel like part of our big Greek family.
