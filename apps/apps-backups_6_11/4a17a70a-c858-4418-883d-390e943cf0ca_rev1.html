About Our Cafe

Welcome to Cat's off to You, Paris's premier destination for coffee, pastries, and purrs! Nestled in a cozy corner with a view of the city, our cafe offers a unique experience where you can relax, work, or socialize in the company of our lovely resident felines. We are dedicated to providing a comfortable and safe environment for both our human and animal guests.

Our cats are rescues, carefully selected for their friendly temperaments, and they are the heart of our cafe. Each visit helps support their well-being and provides them with a loving home until they potentially find their forever family (some are available for adoption!). Come, sip a latte, enjoy a croissant, and let the stress melt away with a gentle purr.

Cat Cafe Etiquette

To ensure a pleasant experience for both our human and feline friends, please observe the following rules:

  • Always approach cats slowly and let them come to you.
  • Do not pick up cats unless they initiate contact and seem comfortable.
  • Avoid waking a sleeping cat. Let them rest!
  • Do not feed the cats human food. We provide approved treats you can purchase.
  • Supervise children closely and ensure they interact gently.
  • Flash photography is discouraged as it can startle the cats.
  • Sanitize your hands upon entering and leaving the cat area.
  • If a cat is hiding or seems stressed, give them space.

Thank you for helping us maintain a calm and happy environment for our resident cats!
