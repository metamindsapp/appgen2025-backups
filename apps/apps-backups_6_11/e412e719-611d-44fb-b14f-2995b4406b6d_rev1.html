Our Story

From Our Family Kitchen to Your Plate

Gene's Beans started with a simple passion: crafting the perfect bowl of chili. Our founder, Gene, spent years perfecting his recipes, drawing inspiration from family traditions and global flavors. What began as a weekend hobby for friends and family soon blossomed into a dream to share this delicious comfort food with the entire Lower Mainland.

We believe in using only the freshest, high-quality ingredients, locally sourced whenever possible. Every batch of chili is made with love, slow-simmered to perfection, ensuring a rich, hearty, and unforgettable experience. From classic beef to adventurous veggie options, there's a bowl for everyone at Gene's Beans.

Our chili truck isn't just about serving food; it's about bringing people together, one warm, satisfying bowl at a time. We're proud to be a part of the vibrant BC food scene and look forward to seeing you at our next stop!
