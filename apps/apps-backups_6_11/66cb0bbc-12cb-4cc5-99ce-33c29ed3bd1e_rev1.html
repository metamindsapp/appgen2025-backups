About Greg's Fences

With years of experience serving the beautiful community of Sooke, BC, Greg's Fences is dedicated to providing top-quality fence solutions. We believe in craftsmanship, reliability, and customer satisfaction above all else.

From robust privacy fences to elegant ornamental designs, our team works closely with you to understand your vision and deliver a fence that not only meets your needs but exceeds your expectations. We use durable materials and proven techniques to ensure your fence stands strong for years to come.

Our commitment to the Sooke community drives us to offer personalized service and build lasting relationships with our clients. Let us help you define your space with a perfect fence.

Our Fence Services

We install all kinds of fences, ensuring the perfect fit for your property and lifestyle.

Wood Fences

Classic and versatile, wood fences offer natural beauty and excellent privacy. We build custom wood fences, including picket, privacy, and post-and-rail styles, using high-quality, weather-resistant timber.

Vinyl Fences

Low maintenance and incredibly durable, vinyl fences are a popular choice for their clean look and longevity. Available in various styles and colors, they won't rot, warp, or splinter.

Chain Link Fences

An economical and robust solution for security and containment. Ideal for backyards, sports fields, and commercial properties, chain link fences are practical and long-lasting.

Ornamental Fences

Add elegance and curb appeal with an ornamental fence. These decorative fences, often made from aluminum or steel, provide a sophisticated look while maintaining security.

Custom Fence Solutions

Have a unique vision or a challenging property? We offer custom fence design and installation services to bring your specific ideas to life, ensuring a perfect fit and finish.

Fence Repair & Maintenance

Beyond installation, we also provide repair and maintenance services to keep your existing fence in top condition, extending its lifespan and preserving its appearance.
